25-27 April 2024

UNITED BY SKILLS

Just for the thrill of it

XXIX Baltic Games for Vocational Schools take place in May 2024. Athletes from Estonia, Latvia, and Lithuania compete in basketball, volleyball, and table tennis. Proudly presented by the Estonian Vocational School Sports Federation in partnership with VOCO.

Join the celebration of Baltic talent and sportsmanship!
